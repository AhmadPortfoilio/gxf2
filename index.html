FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my services, processes.

What information do you need to start?

To get started, I typically need your channel name, preferred style references, any specific colors or branding elements you want included, and the title/text you'd like featured on the thumbnail. The more details you can provide about your vision, the better I can create something you'll love.

How much do you charge for a thumbnail?

My thumbnail designs start at $25 for a basic design. Premium custom thumbnails with more complex graphics and effects range from $40-$60 depending on complexity. I also offer package deals for multiple thumbnails. Contact me for exact pricing based on your specific needs.

How long does it take to complete a thumbnail?

Standard delivery time is 24-48 hours after receiving all necessary information. Rush delivery (within 12 hours) is available for an additional 20% fee. Complex designs may require more time, which I'll discuss with you before starting.

Can I request revisions?

Yes! I include up to 2 rounds of revisions with every design to ensure you're completely satisfied. Additional revisions are available for a small fee. Most clients are happy with the design after 1 revision or none at all.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by contacting me directly through email or my social media channels. I'll send you a brief questionnaire to understand your needs, then provide a quote and timeline. After approval and payment, I'll begin working on your design.

Can you match a specific style?

Absolutely! I specialize in adapting to different styles. Just provide examples of thumbnails or designs you like, and I'll create something that matches your preferred aesthetic while making sure it stands out and represents your brand effectively.
